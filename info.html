About this counter.

When eating or consuming animal products, it is all to easy to disconnect from the astronomical impact these actions have on the animals suffering to provide these products. This counter is here to serve as a visceral reminder of the second-by-second ramifications of our gluttonous animal agriculture practices.

Where does this information come from?

The information is all availble publicy as reported by various industry organizations.
